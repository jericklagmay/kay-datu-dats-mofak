About Hunter x Hunter

*Hunter x Hunter* is a manga and anime series created by Yoshihiro Togashi. It follows the story of Gon Freecss, a young boy who aspires to become a Hunter and find his father.

Popular Characters

Gon Freecss

Gon is the main protagonist, known for his boundless energy, determination, and his goal to find his father, Ging Freecss.

Killua Zoldyck

Killua is Gon's best friend and a skilled assassin from the Zoldyck family. He is known for his quick thinking and loyalty.

Kurapika

Kurapika is the last surviving member of the Kurta Clan, determined to avenge his people and retrieve their stolen eyes.

Leorio Paradinight

Leorio is a loyal friend and aspiring doctor who values friendship and has a strong sense of justice.
